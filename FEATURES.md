# Features - GNPS Learning Hub

GNPS Learning Hub is designed to make learning Punjabi engaging and fun for children through gamification and interactive lessons.

## 🎓 Learning Journey
- **Structured Path**: Progress through a curated journey of lessons, from basic alphabets to complex sentences.
- **Interactive Lessons**: Each lesson consists of multiple tasks designed to reinforce learning through different modalities.
- **Diverse Task Types**:
  - **Tracing**: Practice writing Punjabi characters (Trace).
  - **Spelling**: Build words by selecting the correct letters.
  - **Word Selection**: Match Punjabi words with their meanings or images.
  - **Sentence Building**: Arrange words in the correct order to form sentences.
  - **Fill in the Blanks**: Complete sentences with the appropriate vocabulary.

## 🎮 Gamification & Engagement
- **Gems & Rewards**: Earn gems for every lesson completed. The more you learn, the more you earn!
- **Word Games**: Play word based puzzles and games.
- **Daily Streaks**: Stay motivated with daily streaks. Build a habit by learning every day.
- **Milestones**: Celebrate progress as you unlock new stages of your learning journey.

## 👤 Personalization
- **Avatar System**: Choose your character (Boy or Girl) and customize their appearance.
- **In-App Shop**: Use your hard-earned gems to buy new accessories and outfits for your avatar.
- **Goal Setting**: Set daily learning goals to match your pace (e.g., Casual, Regular, Serious, Intense).

## 🔊 Audio & Multimedia
- **Text-to-Speech (TTS)**: Hear the correct pronunciation of Punjabi words and sentences.
- **Immersive Sounds**: Feedback sounds for success and completion to keep learners engaged.
- **SVG Graphics**: High-quality, scalable vector graphics for a clean and colorful user interface.

## 🛠 Technology & Architecture
- **Offline First**: Uses Hive for local storage, allowing progress to be saved even without an internet connection.
- **Firebase Integration**: Optional Firestore integration for syncing content and potentially backing up progress.
- **State Management**: Powered by Riverpod for a robust and testable codebase.
- **Dynamic Theming**: The app theme color adjusts based on the user's progress and achievements.
